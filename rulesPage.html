How to play Number Game.

Entry fee is $1 to Hallie Day.
Winner takes all.
Get as many points as you can.
Picture or video proof necessary of completion.
Send proof to Hallie, 701-630-0768.

Points:

Worth 1 point:
Get an ice cream cone.
Make a new friend.

Worth 5 points:
Slow dance with someone.
Get asked for number.
Get a compliment.
Get picture with counselor.

Worth 8 points.
Ask for someone's number.
Ask someone to dance.
Give someone of the opposite gender a compliment.

Worth 10 points:
Go to a complete stranger and yell: "Use code emmy10 for 10% off."
Kiss someone.
Get the most fire photo (Send to Hallie Day to be judged).
Sneak out of dorm room.
Participate in talent show.
Participate in music program.
Win a game of spoons.
